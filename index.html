What is XR?

eXtended Reality (XR) is an umbrella term that covers both Virtual Reality (VR), Augmented Reality (AR) and Mixed Reality (MR). We utilize the latest technology and AI to create these experiences, this is done using Unreal Engine 5 and Unity Game Engine. We have examples all over plus people lots more to come in the coming days as we are just getting started.
